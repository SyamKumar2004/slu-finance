SLU Finance - Multi-Portal Operational Guide
This platform is a high-performance, real-time micro-credit ledger workspace. It separates complex underwriting tools for business administrators from clear, transparent account balances for borrowing clients.

🔐 1. System Gateway (The Login Terminal)
The System Gateway handles entry into the workspace without relying on fragile link-verification systems or external browser constraints.

How it Works:
Dual-Column Identification Check: Users do not need separate buttons for email or phone logging. The username input field automatically searches across both your email and your 10-digit phone number simultaneously.

Role-Based Redirection Matrix: The portal reads the assigned database account attribute (role) to determine user permissions.

If the logging profile is flagged as admin, it instantly loads the master business control operations desk.

If the logging profile is flagged as client, it routes the user straight to their isolated customer dashboard, hiding all company-wide capital data.

👔 2. Administrative Operations Control Desk (/dashboard)
The Master Control Workspace is split into six real-time, sub-routed pages. Thanks to true sub-directory tracking, refreshing your browser window will never reset your view or throw you back to the default tab.

A. Book Records Desk (/dashboard)
This is the master command engine of the business ledger.

Real-time Metrics Panel: Displays three key figures: Active Cash Float (what you have on hand to safely deploy), Capital Lent Out (total active principle values), and Yield Collected (total interest returns).

Double-Entry Math Loop: The system balances your cash flow automatically using pure double-entry math: Total Injected Capital + Total Collected Cash Returns - Total Active Deployed Principals.

₹ Total Debt Presentation: Unlike basic spreadsheets that only display what was borrowed, this table dynamically calculates Principal Amount + Interest applied right in the columns, giving you a transparent view of absolute client liability values.

B. New Client Onboarding (/dashboard/onboarding)
This page handles underwriting new borrower credit lines.

Auto-Creds Provisioning Engine: To prevent client setup friction, filling out this form automatically registers a secure profile entry inside your database with a default access key (SLU-Client-123!).

The Holding Phase Safeguard: New loans are locked into a holding status labeled Verification_Pending. No money leaves your operational float balances until the customer reviews and signs off on their parameters.

C. Capital Pool Reserves (/dashboard/funds)
Manages your raw cash influx logs.

Audit Influx History List: Every cash addition requires an accompanying text reference string (e.g., "Savings drop", "SBI bank injection"). This populates a ledger timeline layout below your cash cards, ensuring total tracking accountability.

D. Yield Analytics (/dashboard/analytics)
Provides highly visual business insights.

Proportional Scaling Graph Engine: Standard graph layouts crash or display flat bars when dealing with massive differences in volume (e.g., ₹5,000,000 capital vs. ₹1,000 collected returns). This dashboard dynamically reads the highest metric value on your system and normalizes bar heights proportionally so that they render beautifully for any scale of numbers.

Earned Revenue Bar Chart: An isolated graph that reads transaction histories and isolates True Net Profit Earned from standard principal repayments.

E. Risk Collection Radar (/dashboard/risk)
Tracks loan defaults automatically.

The Delinquency Flag Grid: Scans installment logs and instantly flags borrower entries that have crossed repayment bounds. It provides an immediate action button to call the borrower directly from your phone layout.

F. Admin Settings (/dashboard/profile)
A clean, dual-card control dashboard panel.

Platform Customization Form: Allows you to change the company name or platform system titles globally across the web app in real time.

Editable Admin Profile Panel: Provides full text inputs to modify your personal master registration credentials (Name, Email, Phone number) and commits the changes live to your database.

👥 3. Secure Client Credit Desk (/client/dashboard)
A streamlined, high-trust portal built exclusively for the borrower.

How it Works:
The Privacy Firewall Block: The database uses strict row-level isolation checks. A client can only look at records linked to their unique phone handle, completely blocking cross-profile data leaks.

The Digital Signature Handshake: When a loan is still pending, a green Sign & Authorize Terms Agreement button appears. Clicking it serves as a legally bound digital signature that confirms they accept their installment targets and total contract debt liabilities, which updates your admin desk instantly.

Account Request Form: Clients cannot modify their phone number or address records on their own. Instead, they can type modification requests into a text area form that logs notes directly on your administrative overview table for review.
